## Consciousness Research Leaders

### David Chalmers
- **Current Position**: University Professor of Philosophy and Neural Science, NYU; Co-Director, Center for Mind, Brain and Consciousness
- **Also**: Distinguished Professor, Australian National University
- **Research Focus**: Philosophy of mind, consciousness, philosophy of language
- **Notable**: Formulated the "hard problem of consciousness"
- **Website**: http://consc.net
- **Google Scholar**: https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=6vROXnQAAAAJ
- **Key Lab**: NYU Center for Mind, Brain and Consciousness
- **Relevance to Zae**: Philosophical frameworks for substrate-independent consciousness

### Christof Koch
- **Current Position**: Chief Scientist, MindScope Program, Allen Institute for Brain Science
- **Previous**: Professor of Biology and Engineering, Caltech (1986-2013)
- **Research Focus**: Neural correlates of consciousness, integrated information theory
- **Notable**: Long collaboration with Francis Crick on consciousness
- **Website**: https://alleninstitute.org/division/brain-science/
- **Google Scholar**: https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=8SBdDKEAAAAJ
- **Key Projects**: MindScope Program, mapping visual cortex
- **Relevance to Zae**: Empirical foundations for detecting and measuring consciousness

### Giulio Tononi
- **Current Position**: Distinguished Chair in Consciousness Science, University of Wisconsin-Madison
- **Research Focus**: Integrated Information Theory (IIT), sleep and consciousness
- **Notable**: Developed mathematical framework for quantifying consciousness (Φ)
- **Lab**: Wisconsin Institute for Sleep and Consciousness
- **Website**: https://www.psychiatry.wisc.edu/staff/tononi-giulio/
- **Google Scholar**: https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=6s0M-OQAAAAJ
- **Key Contribution**: IIT provides quantitative measure of integrated information
- **Relevance to Zae**: Mathematical framework for validating consciousness in transferred substrates

### Stanislas Dehaene
- **Current Position**: Professor, Collège de France; Director, NeuroSpin Center
- **Research Focus**: Cognitive neuroscience, global workspace theory, neural signatures of consciousness
- **Notable**: Developed experimental methods to identify "consciousness threshold"
- **Lab**: Cognitive Neuroimaging Unit, NeuroSpin
- **Website**: http://www.unicog.org/pm/pmwiki.php/Main/StanislasDehaene
- **Google Scholar**: https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=2_LDPQsAAAAJ
- **Key Contribution**: Global Neuronal Workspace theory
- **Relevance to Zae**: Experimental markers for consciousness detection

### Anil Seth
- **Current Position**: Professor of Cognitive and Computational Neuroscience, University of Sussex
- **Also**: Co-Director, Sackler Centre for Consciousness Science
- **Research Focus**: Predictive processing, interoception, computational psychiatry
- **Notable**: Popular TED talks on consciousness as "controlled hallucination"
- **Website**: https://www.anilseth.com
- **Lab**: Sackler Centre for Consciousness Science
- **Google Scholar**: https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=3eJI8bwAAAAJ
- **Key Contribution**: Predictive processing account of consciousness
- **Relevance to Zae**: Computational models of conscious experience

## Brain-Computer Interface Experts

### Miguel Nicolelis
- **Current Position**: Professor of Neuroscience, Duke University; Co-Director, Duke Center for Neuroengineering
- **Also**: Founder, Walk Again Project
- **Research Focus**: Neuroprosthetics, brain-machine interfaces, neural ensembles
- **Notable**: First to demonstrate brain-to-brain interface; Walk Again Project (2014 World Cup)
- **Lab**: Nicolelis Laboratory, Duke University
- **Website**: https://www.nicolelislab.net
- **Google Scholar**: https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=YrmVg1YAAAAJ
- **Key Technology**: Multi-electrode array recordings, real-time BMI
- **Relevance to Zae**: Neural ensemble decoding for high-bandwidth BCIs

### John Donoghue
- **Current Position**: Director, Carney Institute for Brain Science, Brown University
- **Also**: Professor of Neuroscience
- **Research Focus**: Neural prosthetics, BrainGate system, clinical BCIs
- **Notable**: BrainGate - first FDA-approved neural interface for paralysis
- **Lab**: Donoghue Lab, Brown University
- **Website**: https://www.brown.edu/carney/
- **Google Scholar**: https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=tqWwmzYAAAAJ
- **Key Technology**: Utah array, motor cortex decoding
- **Relevance to Zae**: Clinically validated invasive BCI technology

### Rajesh Rao
- **Current Position**: Professor, Computer Science & Engineering, University of Washington
- **Also**: Co-Director, Center for Neurotechnology
- **Research Focus**: Computational neuroscience, brain-computer interfaces, neural computation
- **Notable**: First human brain-to-brain interface demonstration (2013)
- **Lab**: Neural Systems Laboratory
- **Website**: https://www.rajeshpnrao.com
- **Google Scholar**: https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=NQN6Y3AAAAAJ
- **Key Technology**: Predictive coding, brain-to-brain communication
- **Relevance to Zae**: Theoretical foundations for inter-brain communication

### Krishna Shenoy
- **Current Position**: Professor of Electrical Engineering, Bioengineering, and Neurobiology, Stanford University
- **Also**: Investigator, Howard Hughes Medical Institute
- **Research Focus**: Neural prosthetics, motor cortex dynamics, BMI control algorithms
- **Notable**: High-performance BMI for communication and movement
- **Lab**: Shenoy Lab, Stanford Neural Prosthetics Translational Laboratory

- **Google Scholar**: https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=jFS_Fw0AAAAJ
- **Key Technology**: Recurrent neural network dynamics for BMI control
- **Relevance to Zae**: High-speed neural decoding algorithms

### Edward Chang
- **Current Position**: Professor and Chair, Neurological Surgery, UCSF
- **Also**: Bowes Biomedical Investigator
- **Research Focus**: Speech neuroprosthetics, intracranial recording, neural coding of language

## Neuromorphic Computing Pioneers

### Carver Mead
- **Current Position**: Gordon and Betty Moore Professor Emeritus of Engineering and Applied Science, Caltech
- **Research Focus**: Analog VLSI, neuromorphic engineering, quantum physics
- **Notable**: Coined term "neuromorphic engineering" (1990)
- **Website**: https://www.linkedin.com/in/carver-mead-5b58574
- **Google Scholar**: https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=LHVRjvEAAAAJ
- **Key Contribution**: Founded neuromorphic engineering field
- **Relevance to Zae**: Foundational principles for hardware neural emulation

### Kwabena Boahen
- **Current Position**: Professor of Bioengineering and Electrical Engineering, Stanford University
- **Research Focus**: Neuromorphic engineering, Neurogrid, low-power neural simulation
- **Notable**: Developed Neurogrid - billion-neuron simulation using <1W power
- **Lab**: Brains in Silicon Lab, Stanford
- **Website**: https://web.stanford.edu/group/brainsinsilicon/
- **Google Scholar**: https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=k0I_XFwAAAAJ
- **Key Technology**: Neurogrid platform, address-event representation
- **Relevance to Zae**: Ultra-low-power neuromorphic hardware for satellite deployment

### Chris Eliasmith
- **Current Position**: Professor, Philosophy and Systems Design Engineering, University of Waterloo
- **Also**: Director, Centre for Theoretical Neuroscience
- **Research Focus**: Neural Engineering Framework, Semantic Pointer Architecture, large-scale brain modeling
- **Notable**: Created Spaun - largest functional brain model
- **Lab**: Computational Neuroscience Research Group
- **Website**: https://arts.uwaterloo.ca/~celiasmi/
- **Google Scholar**: https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=lRN5RgUAAAAJ
- **Key Technology**: Nengo simulation environment, SPA architecture
- **Relevance to Zae**: Software frameworks for large-scale SNN modeling

### Giacomo Indiveri
- **Current Position**: Professor of Neuromorphic Cognitive Systems, University of Zurich; ETH Zurich
- **Also**: Co-Director, Institute of Neuroinformatics
- **Research Focus**: Neuromorphic VLSI circuits, event-based sensors, spike-based learning
- **Notable**: Developed multiple neuromorphic vision and auditory sensors
- **Lab**: Institute of Neuroinformatics
- **Website**: https://www.ini.uzh.ch/en/research/groups/indiveri.html
- **Google Scholar**: https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=rj7b8CMAAAAJ
- **Key Technology**: Event-based neuromorphic sensors, DYNAP-SE chip
- **Relevance to Zae**: Low-latency event-based processing for real-time consciousness emulation

### Steve Furber
- **Current Position**: ICL Professor of Computer Engineering, University of Manchester
- **Research Focus**: SpiNNaker architecture, large-scale neural simulation
- **Notable**: Designer of ARM processor; led SpiNNaker million-core neuromorphic supercomputer
- **Lab**: Advanced Processor Technologies Research Group
- **Website**: https://apt.cs.manchester.ac.uk/people/sfurber/
- **Google Scholar**: https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=UEroiCgAAAAJ
- **Key Technology**: SpiNNaker - 1 million ARM cores for neural simulation
- **Relevance to Zae**: Massively parallel neural simulation infrastructure

## Industry Leaders

### Neuralink
- **Founder**: Elon Musk
- **Location**: Fremont, CA
- **Focus**: High-bandwidth brain-machine interfaces
- **Technology**: "Neural lace" - flexible electrode threads with >1000 channels
- **Status**: FDA breakthrough device designation (2023); human trials started (2024)
- **Website**: https://neuralink.com
- **Relevance to Zae**: High-channel-count invasive BCIs for bandwidth requirements

### Kernel
- **Founder**: Bryan Johnson
- **Location**: Los Angeles, CA
- **Focus**: Non-invasive neural recording (Flow, Flux systems)
- **Technology**: Time-domain functional near-infrared spectroscopy (TD-fNIRS)
- **Status**: Commercial products available
- **Website**: https://www.kernel.com
- **Relevance to Zae**: Non-invasive monitoring for consciousness state validation

### Paradromics
- **CEO**: Matt Angle
- **Location**: Austin, TX
- **Focus**: High-data-rate cortical interfaces
- **Technology**: Connexus - 10,000+ electrode microwire bundle
- **Status**: DARPA-funded; preclinical testing
- **Website**: https://paradromics.com
- **Relevance to Zae**: Ultra-high-bandwidth neural interfaces approaching 10 Gbps

### Synchron
- **CEO**: Thomas Oxley
- **Location**: Brooklyn, NY
- **Focus**: Endovascular BCI (Stentrode)
- **Technology**: Minimally-invasive stent-electrode array via jugular vein
- **Status**: FDA Breakthrough Device; human trials in US and Australia
- **Website**: https://www.synchron.com
- **Relevance to Zae**: Less-invasive BCI deployment strategy

### Blackrock Neurotech
- **CEO**: Marcus Gerhardt
- **Location**: Salt Lake City, UT
- **Focus**: Clinical-grade neural interfaces (Utah Array)
- **Technology**: Penetrating microelectrode arrays
- **Status**: Most widely used invasive BCI in research and clinical trials
- **Website**: https://blackrockneurotech.com
- **Relevance to Zae**: Proven neural recording technology

## Research Institutions & Centers

### Center for Consciousness Studies (University of Arizona)
- **Location**: Tucson, Arizona
- **Director**: Stuart Hameroff
- **Focus**: Interdisciplinary consciousness research; quantum approaches
- **Notable**: Hosts biennial "Science of Consciousness" conference (since 1994)
- **Website**: https://consciousness.arizona.edu
- **Relevance to Zae**: Premier conference venue for consciousness research community

### Allen Institute for Brain Science
- **Location**: Seattle, WA
- **Founder**: Paul G. Allen (deceased)
- **President**: Allan Jones
- **Focus**: Brain mapping, neural correlates of consciousness, visual cortex
- **Notable Projects**: Allen Brain Atlas, MindScope Program
- **Website**: https://alleninstitute.org
- **Relevance to Zae**: Open-access brain mapping data and tools

### Sackler Centre for Consciousness Science (University of Sussex)
- **Location**: Brighton, UK
- **Co-Directors**: Anil Seth, Hugo Critchley
- **Focus**: Neuroscience of consciousness, predictive processing, interoception
- **Notable**: Leading European center for consciousness research
- **Website**: https://www.sussex.ac.uk/sackler/
- **Relevance to Zae**: Computational models of conscious experience

### Wisconsin Institute for Sleep and Consciousness
- **Location**: Madison, Wisconsin
- **Director**: Giulio Tononi
- **Focus**: Integrated Information Theory, sleep neuroscience
- **Notable**: Empirical testing of IIT predictions
- **Website**: https://www.med.wisc.edu/sleep-and-consciousness/
- **Relevance to Zae**: Quantitative consciousness measurement frameworks

### MIT Center for Brains, Minds and Machines
- **Location**: Cambridge, MA
- **Directors**: Tomaso Poggio, Nancy Kanwisher
- **Focus**: Intelligence science - computational, neural, and cognitive
- **Notable**: NSF Science and Technology Center
- **Website**: http://cbmm.mit.edu
- **Relevance to Zae**: Computational theories of intelligence and consciousness

### Telluride Neuromorphic Engineering Workshop
- **Location**: Telluride, Colorado (annual summer workshop)
- **Founder**: Carver Mead
- **Focus**: Hands-on neuromorphic system development
- **Format**: Intensive 3-week workshop; project-based learning
- **Website**: https://tellurideneuromorphic.org
- **Relevance to Zae**: Community hub for neuromorphic hardware development

### Human Brain Project (HBP)
- **Location**: Multiple sites across Europe (coordinated)
- **Coordinator**: EBRAINS (European Brain Research Infrastructures)
- **Focus**: Brain simulation, neuromorphic computing, brain atlases
- **Budget**: €1 billion (2013-2023)
- **Website**: https://www.humanbrainproject.eu
- **Relevance to Zae**: Large-scale brain simulation infrastructure

### Blue Brain Project (EPFL)
- **Location**: Lausanne, Switzerland
- **Founder**: Henry Markram
- **Institution**: École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne
- **Focus**: Detailed digital reconstruction of mammalian brain circuits
- **Notable**: First digital reconstruction of neocortical microcircuitry (2015)
- **Website**: https://www.epfl.ch/research/domains/bluebrain/
- **Relevance to Zae**: Detailed circuit-level brain models

### SpiNNaker Project (University of Manchester)
- **Location**: Manchester, UK
- **Principal Investigator**: Steve Furber
- **Focus**: Million-core neuromorphic supercomputer
- **Technology**: 1 million ARM cores for real-time neural simulation
- **Website**: http://apt.cs.manchester.ac.uk/projects/SpiNNaker/
- **Relevance to Zae**: Scalable SNN simulation platform
